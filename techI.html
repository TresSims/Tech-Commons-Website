Technology Commons I

Technology Commons I is located right next door to Technology Commons II, and offers a rich suite of amenities for students and faculty to make use of. Tech Commons I, it has a large computer lab with Windows computers, that can be accessed anytime the building is open. They are distributed on a first come first serve basis, so make sure to check below and make sure that there are available seats! Additionally, TCI has larger conference rooms, that may be reserved at the SSD if your organization is planning a meeting.
