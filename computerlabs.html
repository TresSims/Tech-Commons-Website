Computer Labs

At a Glance

The Computer Labs here at UCF offer a rich suite of amenities for students and faculty to make use of. Tech Commons I, Tech Commons II, and Classroom Building I each have a large computer lab, with both Windows & Mac computers, that can be accessed anytime the respective buildings are open. They are distributed on a first come first serve basis, so make sure to click on one of the "Current Vacant Computers" below to view each lab's available seats! Additionally, to reserve a larger conference room, please visit the Student Support Desk located in Technology Commons II.
