Dell Latitude (2014)

A workhorse for students everywhere, the Dell Latitude is a good all around computer that will get you through classes, presentations, and web browsing alike. It makes for a useful all in one laptop, and comes pre-loaded with Microsoft Office. In addition, we will provide a charger to use for the rental period. These laptops are rented out for 24 hours and MUST be returned at the end of that period. If Technology Commons will be closed when your item is due, you MUST return the device 30 minutes before close on the day before Technology Commons is closed.

Visit the Student Support Desk in either Tech Commons II or Classroom Building I to find out about availability.
