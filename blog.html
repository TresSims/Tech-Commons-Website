Spring 2017 - Week 3 Update

January 23rd, 2017

Welcome back to class Knights! Hopefully you are all settled into your classes and looking forward to the semester! We have some big things happening here at Tech Commons which I am excited to share with you. First, we now have brand new Surface Laptops available for check out from the Student Support Desk in TCII! They come with a pen, charger, and are pre-loaded with Windows 10 along with the software you guys are used to seeing on our Dell computers. Additionally, we recently got our brand new 3D printer! We don’t have it quite set up yet, but we are super excited to have that up and running. So make sure you keep checking Facebook and our website for more information about that.

That’s all from us for now Knights! Good luck this semester, and stay connected!
