Adapters & Cables

Rentals include Mini DisplayPort out to video inputs such as VGA, DVI, and HDMI; mini USB, USB-A, and USB-C may be rented as well. These are only rented out until the end of the day, and our due back 30 minutes before the Student Support Desk closes.

Visit the Student Support Desk in either Tech Common II or Classroom Building I to find out about availability.
