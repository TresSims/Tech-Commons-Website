Go Pro Hero 4

The Go Pro Hero 4 is one of the most high quality rugged cameras available, capable of capturing HD video while remaining waterproof up to 40M And shock and dirt resistant. It's capable of going where you are and recording anything and everything along the way.

The GoPro may be rented out for 3 days, unless the due time falls on a day that Technology Commons is closed. In these instances, you will be required to return your rental 30 minutes before close the day before.
