Microsoft Surface

The Mircrosoft Surface is a great 2-in-1 tablet computer that can handle any of your needs, from illustration, to note taking, to browsing the web. The rental includes a charger and pen that must be returned with the tablet. These tablets are rented out for 24 hours and MUST be returned at the end of that period. If Technology Commons will be closed when your item is due, you MUST return the device 30 minutes before close on the day before Technology Commons is closed.

Visit the Student Support Desk in either Tech Commons II or Classroom Building I to find out about availability.
