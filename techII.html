Technology Commons II

Technology Commons II is the hub for all things technical here at UCF. In the lobby, we have both Cafe Bustelo, and the BYoT(Bring your own Tech). It's a perfect place for students to come in, work on homework, and generally socialize. Down the hall, we have a large computer lab, with both Mac and PC devices. All of these computers are first come, first serve, so make sure to check below to ensure there are computers available! If you're looking for something more private, or need a place to record some video, we also have study rooms available to rent from the student support desk.
